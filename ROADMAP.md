# 🗺 Nexora Roadmap

Welcome to the Nexora roadmap.

This document outlines our long-term vision, milestones, and future plans. Nexora is designed to evolve from an open-source GitHub repository into a comprehensive engineering knowledge platform.

---

# 🎯 Vision

Build the world's most trusted, practical, and community-driven engineering knowledge platform.

Our goal is to make high-quality engineering education freely accessible to everyone.

---

# 🚀 Core Principles

- 🌍 Free Forever
- 🔓 Open Source
- 📚 Community Driven
- 💻 Practical Learning
- 🎯 Production-Ready Content
- 🔄 Continuous Improvement
