# 🤝 Nexora Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Nexora a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of age, experience level, nationality, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, education, or background.

Our goal is to build an open-source community where people can learn, collaborate, and grow together.

---

# 🌍 Our Values

As members of the Nexora community, we strive to:

- 🤝 Treat everyone with respect.
- 📚 Share knowledge openly.
- 💬 Welcome constructive discussions.
- 🌱 Help beginners learn.
- 🚀 Encourage continuous improvement.
- ❤️ Build a positive and supportive community.

---

# ✅ Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Provide helpful reviews.
- Focus on improving the project.
- Respect different opinions and experiences.
- Credit others for their work.
- Keep discussions relevant and productive.

---

# ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or bullying
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech
- Discrimination
- Threats or intimidation
- Trolling
- Deliberate misinformation
- Sharing private information without permission
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to the project

---

# 📚 Content Standards

All documentation should:

- Be accurate.
- Be respectful.
- Be practical.
- Avoid plagiarism.
- Credit external sources when necessary.
- Follow Nexora's documentation standards.

---

# 💬 Communication

We encourage:

- Asking questions.
- Sharing ideas.
- Giving constructive feedback.
- Respectful technical discussions.
- Helping other contributors.

Disagreements are natural.

Handle them professionally.

---

# 🛡 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers.

Provide:

- A description of the incident
- Relevant screenshots (if applicable)
- Links or references
- Any additional context

Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially.

---

# ⚖ Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- Friendly reminders
- Requesting changes
- Removing inappropriate content
- Temporarily restricting participation
- Permanent removal from the community for repeated or severe violations

Actions will always be based on maintaining a healthy and respectful community.

---

# 🌱 Our Community Philosophy

Nexora exists to make engineering knowledge freely accessible.

We believe:

> Learn openly.

> Teach generously.

> Collaborate respectfully.

Knowledge grows when it is shared.

---

# ❤️ Thank You

Thank you for helping make Nexora a welcoming place for engineers, students, educators, and contributors around the world.

Together, we're building more than documentation.

We're building a global engineering community.
